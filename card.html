Vizsla

The mighty Vizsla belongs to the FCI group 7 (the pointer group).

  • Domestic dog: lupus familiaris
  • Female Height: 21–24 in (53–61 cm)
  • Female Weight: 40–55 lb (18–25 kg)
  • Temperament: Loves kids and playing

An example Vizsla is Duke Goggin. Duke enjoys: cuddling under a warm blanket, going for walks, and eating.
